Nessa Carson: About

I am a professional synthetic organic chemist working in drug discovery in south-east England. In my spare time, I like to get involved with a number of other science-related projects. A computer scientist friend told me that the best way to learn to code was to start with markup by producing a personal website. I then wrote this website without prior knowledge of CSS or JavaScript. The site's purpose is as a repository for content I think is useful, mostly to scientists. I also write poetry.

Some interesting projects I'm involved with:

OUSS logo

Oxford University Scientific Society

OUSS is the world's oldest, student-run scientific society. As the Vice President, I am responsible for inviting internationally-renowned scientists to share their knowledge with us, in the form of seminars. I am committed to ensuring that talks remain open to the general public. I also maintain the Facebook page (198,000 likes).

Freelance Writing

I enjoy writing science-based magazine articles for technical audiences. I write for the Royal Society of Chemistry's magazine Chemistry World.
