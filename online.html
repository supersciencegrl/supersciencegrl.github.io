Online chemistry calendar

Having featured 1244 webinars, conferences, and training courses, the online chemistry event database is now closed. With many thanks to Khoi Van for creating the auto-timezone conversion feature.

The new list can now be found over at researchseminars.org, a new database administered by MIT academics and curated by crowdsourcing.
View and edit their webinar list or conference list, and set the toggle for 'chemistry'!

Additionally, you may wish to:

  • View my conference-only database, which is ongoing
  • Check out @AnywhereChem on Twitter for a rolling feed of online chemistry events
  • See the legacy list of 1244 old webinars
