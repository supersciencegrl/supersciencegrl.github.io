About me

I'm a digital chemist, working with laboratory data and workflows, with a background in high-throughput experimentation (HTE) and reaction optimization. I like working with lab data ecosystems, and coding in Python and R to make everything that we do easier, better, and more collaborative. I'm also a regular columnist for Chemistry World and a Scientific Advisory Board member at ChemAI and Chemical & Engineering News. I welcome queries about my consultation services on automation and HTE matters for small molecule chemistry.
