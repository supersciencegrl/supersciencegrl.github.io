About me

I'm a synthetic organic chemist, working with laboratory automation for reaction optimization. Although my main focus is chemistry, I like coding in Python and R to automate everything that makes our work easier. I'm also a Scientific Editor at Synthetic Reaction Updates by the RSC, and a regular columnist for Chemistry World. I'm always happy to consult on automation and HTE matters for small molecule chemistry. What I bring are stats, bullet points, and teamwork.
